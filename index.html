Car dealership Data Analysis using SQL

I designed and executed a database for a car dealership to maintain records of inventory, sales processes, and customer insights. By recognizing high-spending customers and identifying buying patterns, I investigated customer demographics and frequently visited locations to enable targeted campaigns. Sales analysis included visualizations of monthly and quarterly revenue trends, as well as insights into the best-selling car models and most common payment methods. I evaluated primary costs, selling prices, and servicing expenditures to calculate profit margins and highlighted models that were expensive to maintain, helping optimize inventory choices. Ensuring data integrity, I identified null values and duplicate records to maintain data quality. Using advanced SQL queries, I performed data exploration, summarization, and advanced analytics to unlock actionable insights, with training data up to October 2023. These efforts included leveraging advanced SQL techniques for optimization and in-depth analysis.
